CDAPersistenceManager

@interface CDAPersistenceManager : NSObject

Subclasses of this class manage a persistent store.

A minimal subclass needs to at least override deleteAssetWithIdentifier:, deleteEntryWithIdentifier:, fetchAssetsFromDataStore, fetchAssetWithIdentifier:, fetchEntryWithIdentifier:, fetchSpaceFromDataStore and saveDataStore.
